Flyer Prompt

This is the detailed prompt used to guide the AI in generating the poster design.

"Create a modern, eye-catching social media poster for Northern Threads, a custom T-shirt printing shop based in Jaffna, Sri Lanka.

The design should highlight creativity, youth culture, and local pride.

Include text sections for:
Brand name: Northern Threads
Tagline: (e.g., “Wear Your Story”)
Services: Custom T-shirt Printing for Youth Clubs • Schools • Events
Call to Action: “Order Now!” or “Design Your Own Tee Today!”
Contact Info: Instagram @NorthernThreads | Jaffna, Sri Lanka

Visual style:
Vibrant colors (blue, white, gold, or red accents)
Trendy and youthful typography
Minimal, clean layout with mockups of custom T-shirts
Add subtle thread or fabric textures in the background
Include icons or symbols representing printing, art, or fashion

Aspect Ratio: 1080x1080 px (Instagram post) or 1080x1920 px (Instagram story / reel cover)"
