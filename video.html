Google Veo3 Prompt

This is the detailed prompt used to guide the AI in generating the video.

"Create a short, engaging 8–10 second promotional video for Northern Threads, a custom T-shirt printing shop based in Jaffna, Sri Lanka.

The video should reflect creativity, youthful energy, and local pride, targeting students, youth clubs, and event organizers.

Visual Style & Elements:
Opening scene: vibrant close-up of colorful custom T-shirts being printed or designed.
Mid scene: energetic transitions showing happy youth groups wearing custom Northern Threads shirts at a school event or street hangout.
Ending scene: logo reveal with slogan (e.g., “Wear Your Story”) and call-to-action (“Order Now!”).
Add dynamic camera movement and smooth transitions.
Include thread or fabric animation effects as visual motifs.
Use modern upbeat background music (no lyrics).

Duration: 8–10 seconds.
Aspect Ratio: Vertical 1080×1920 px (for Instagram Reels, TikTok, Facebook)."
